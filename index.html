Vote on April 6th, 2021!

Working to make Eau Claire a better place to live has been a passion of mine for the last 18 years. I have had the pleasure of organizing alongside many great people who’ve helped get us to where we are today. I feel I’ve become a leader who listens and acts upon the ambitions of this community, and I have a track record of helping citizens get involved and make a difference.

A member of City Council since 2018, I am dedicated to District 3, and I have never missed a City Council meeting or a vote. I have also worked to make sure that more people have a voice in our local government, by supporting the creation of the public comment period at all City Council meetings, and at boards, committees, and commissions.

Before joining the City Council, I helped to create the Chippewa Valley Bike Map (2015), the City of Eau Claire Sustainability Advisory Committee (2014), and expanded community gardens and bike and walking trails throughout the city.
